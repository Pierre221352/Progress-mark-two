About Us

SuperSubs is an online Submarine Sandwich ordering service. You can browse the home page to see all the different subs that you can order. You can also go to the Build a Sub! page to create your own custom sub sandwich from a variety of different breads, toppings, sauces and drinks. You can apply a discount code to reduce the overall cost. On the home page you can go to the bottom of the page and see what platforms you can find out more about SuperSubs, and you can head over to the Subs of the Month page to see what are customers favorite subs that were created.
